Land Court Department: Title Examiner in Tax Foreclosure case

G.L. c. 60, § 66

Method of Appointment: By Recorder or Deputy Recorder from list

Qualifications:
1) Member of the Massachusetts Bar for three years or more, and
2) Experience in conveyancing/title examination as evidenced by (a) resume, (b) two letters of recommendation from members of the Massachusetts Bar familiar with the applicant’s work, and (c) sample abstract of title

Method of Application:
Application Form is available on the Land Court web page.
Apply to:
Chief Justice Gordon H. Piper

Land Court Department: Commissioner for Partition of Real Estate

G.L. c. 241, § 12.

Method of Appointment: By Judge from list

Qualifications:
1) Admission to practice before SJC in good standing
2) No felony conviction
3) At least 10 years’ experience practicing in the real estate field, including at least three cases involving the partition of real estate - at least one of which has been completed, either through partition on the ground or a sale
4) Working knowledge of G.L. c. 241
5) Professional liability insurance of at least $100,000

Method of Application:
Application Form is available on the Land Court web page.
Submit to:
Chief Justice Gordon H. Piper
Land Court Department
Three Pemberton Square
Boston, MA 02108
